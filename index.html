Deadly Tsunamis

Higashi Nihon Daishinsai (Tohoku Earthquake) Tsunami

A tremendous tsunami that devastated the coasts of Miyagi, Iwate, Fukushima and other north eastern prefectures in Japan.

Height: 40.5m

2004 Indian Ocean Earthquake Tsunami

The deadliest tsunami in history. Devastated the coasts of atleast a dozen countries from Southeast Asia all the way to East Africa. Atleast 225,000 fatalities.

Height: 30m

1960 Valdivia Earthquake Tsunami

This tsunami was caused by the strongest earthquake in history since 1900. The earthquake was destructive enough with an MMI rating of XII (12) but the tsunami caused MUCH MORE damage, reaching places thousands of kilometers away, Even Russia.

Height: 25m

2004 North Sumatra Earthquake Tsunami

The deadliest tsunami in history. Devastated the coasts of atleast a dozen countries from Southeast Asia all the way to East Africa. Atleast 270,000 fatalities.

Height: 40.5m
